Here is a snippit of my interview. My full interview is featured on Jukebox Mind. Go check them out to find out more!

Q. Welcome to Jukebox Mind! How are you doing?

I’m doing great! I’m so excited for my newest book release and the coming of fall (my favorite season).

Q. Your new book is now available on Amazon. Please tell us about your wonderful book.

‘What Happened to Charlotte?’ is a book about friendship and love. Everyone revolves around Charlotte and her infectious light but when she goes missing, everyone is left feeling lost and determined to find her. It’s a paranormal fiction so expect some spooks!
